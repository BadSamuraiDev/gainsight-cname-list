# Gainsight CNAME List
The .csv was generated performing a lookup on 1300 common tech domains. This is only to provide a __potential__ 3rd-party blast radius while details are becoming available. It is __not__ exhaustive, a condemnation, or indicator of a breach.

### Breach Timeline and Sources

- 2025-11-20: [Salesforce investigates customer data theft via Gainsight breach (bleepingcomputer.com)](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/salesforce-investigates-customer-data-theft-via-gainsight-breach/)

### Please take into consideration:
- The record could be stale.
- They re-used the record for another service.
- The service was a proof-of-value or non-prod.
- An org could have Gainsight under another domain.
